Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available at checkout for an additional charge.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Products must be unused, in original packaging. Contact us to initiate a return.
How do I apply a coupon code?
Add items to your cart, go to checkout, and enter your coupon code in the "Coupon Code" field. Discount will be applied automatically.
Are the jewelry items real/authentic?
All our Chosematics pieces are crafted from high-quality materials. Each product listing clearly states the material used.
Do you offer size exchanges?
Yes! Free size exchanges within 15 days of delivery. Please contact our support team with your order number.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with trusted payment gateways. We never store card details.
